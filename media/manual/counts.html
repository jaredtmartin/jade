Jade - Physical Counts

Physical Counts provide a way to verify and/or correct your inventory. You may want to do a regular physical counts to check that everything is in order. In other cases, you may notice that the quantity of one or more of the items in inventory is not correct. You can use Physical Counts to resolve both of these needs.

Creating

The first step is to create counts for all of the products you want to count. Perhaps you do a complete inventory of everything yearly. You may find it better to count a few items daily or monthly so that by the end of the year, everything is counted.
